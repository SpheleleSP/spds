About Us

SPDS (Pty Ltd) is a proudly South African distribution company based in Ladysmith, KwaZulu-Natal. We specialize in delivering essential goods and supplies to SAPS stations, public schools, clinics, and hospitals in rural areas. Our mission is to fill critical supply gaps and support frontline institutions with speed and reliability.

Our Services

  • Bulk delivery of hygiene products and cleaning supplies
  • Office and stationery distribution
  • Medical supply logistics for clinics and hospitals
  • School furniture, PPE, and essential goods delivery

Who We Serve

We proudly distribute to:

  • South African Police Service (SAPS) stations
  • Government clinics and hospitals
  • Public schools (primary and secondary)
  • Community projects and NGOs
